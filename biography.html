Biography

Swe Swe Aung received her Bachelor of Computer Science (B.C.Sc) in 2004 from the University of Computer Studies, Loikaw, Myanmar, and her Master of Computer Science (M.C.Sc) in 2009 from the University of Computer Studies, Mandalay, Myanmar.”

In 2019, she completed a second Ph.D. in Engineering from the University of the Ryukyus, Okinawa, Japan, with a dissertation titled “A Study on Advanced Applications of Machine Learning Approach and Image Processing: Traffic, Weather, Pattern Recognition, and Noise Filtering.”

She is currently working as a Solution Developer at an international company, where she is actively involved in major projects, including CBPR+ and MEPS+, as well as the development of internal tools such as the LAU Tool and a PDF Reader Tool.

Her research interests include artificial intelligence, machine learning, transportation technologies, image processing, mobile computing, cloud computing, blockchain technologies, and education technology.
